Liam's Bookstore is a store that specializes in delivering you the best in Fiction, Non-Fiction, Graphic Novels, Vinyl records and more to meet all enthusiasts and collectors needs! The store is owned and operated by long time reading fan Liam Borden who used to spend much of his life in the literature industry.

Although he has spent much of his life in the literature industry, he made a change in his life and put his passion and loyalty in books. He has owned this location since 2010 and sells the best of new and used books, comics and more!
